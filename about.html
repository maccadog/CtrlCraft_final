About CtrlCraft

Where precision engineering meets artistic excellence in gaming controller customization

Our Story

CtrlCraft is New Zealand's premier custom controller specialists, born from a passion for gaming and precision engineering. Founded by a team of aerospace manufacturing specialists and gaming enthusiasts right here in Aotearoa, we recognized the gap between mass-produced controllers and the level of quality that serious Kiwi gamers deserve.

Our journey began in professional spray booths and vacuum chambers, where we applied the same rigorous standards used in aerospace components to gaming controllers. The result is a level of finish and durability that exceeds even the original manufacturers.

Today, we're proud to serve gamers across New Zealand, from casual enthusiasts to professional esports athletes, delivering custom controllers that don't just look amazing‚Äîthey perform at the highest level. As a Kiwi-owned business, we understand the unique needs of New Zealand gamers and provide local expertise with fast nationwide delivery.
